## Prove Bijection

* prove bijection 的方法： 
    * find a inverse $g$
        * $g \circ f = f \circ  g =id$, then $g = f^{-1}$



## Well-defined

* For operation $O$

    * Let $a_1 = a_2, b_1 = b_2$
    * Prove $a_1Oa_2  = b_1Ob_2$

* For function $f$

    * Let $x = y$
    * Prove $f(x) = f(y)$

    

## Uniqueness

* For function
    * Assume $f$ and $f'$ both satisfies....
    * Prove $f(x) = f'(x)$ for all $x$

## Prove Subgroups

1. normal way: identity, inverse, closure
2. $\forall a,b \in H, b^{-1}a\in H$
